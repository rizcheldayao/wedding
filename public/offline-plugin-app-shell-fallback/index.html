- Our story -

Future Mr. & Mrs. Wood

Oliver and Rizchel first met in Austin, TX in July 2016. Oliver took Rizchel wine tasting at the Oasis for their first date (best date ever!). It didnt take long after that until we spent most of our time together and we planned a trip to Nashville about a week after we started dating (crazy huh but so worth it). Nashville was just the start of our Wanderlust where in short space of time we visited seven countries and countless cities including the beautiful cities of Waco, Texas and Birmingham, Alabama! Our fondness of each other progressed and we made our first large commitment - we adopted little Autumn, the fluffiest puppy in the whole of Austin! Soon after we took another big step to setting a date and decided to move into a beautiful apartment on the outskirts of gentrification. For Olivers 30th Birthday Rizchel surprised him with a trip to Mexico, where some could say hed reached the peak of his physical condition and was in the shape of his life. However, the biggest surprise was yet to come as Oliver had planned a proposal that not even Harry or Meghan could compete with. On a hot Winter's night in Cancun Olivers elaborate plan came together with the help of family and friends and he proposed to the love of his life. However, after a countless of pranks through their relationship Rizchel thought this was yet another prank After a long and emotional speech she realized that this was for reals and said hell yeh! Since our engagement we have moved to the green chile state - Colorado where we our building our future lives together in sometimes heavy snow and sometimes in tropical sunshine! We cant wait to take the next step of our lives together with all our friends and family in 2019 in California, hope to see you all there.
